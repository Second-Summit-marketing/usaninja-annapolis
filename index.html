🥷 What Is USA Ninja Challenge?

USA Ninja Challenge is a nationwide network of obstacle course training gyms designed specifically for kids. Think American Ninja Warrior — but built for ages 3 to 17, with trained coaches, structured curriculum, and a focus on building confidence, strength, and grit.

We're not a trampoline park or a drop-in play center. Our progressive class curriculum teaches real skills — grip strength, balance, agility, and problem-solving — in a safe, coached environment where every kid is challenged at their level.

Annapolis will be our newest location, and we're building it from the ground up at 441 Defense Highway — a 7,000+ sq ft facility with competition-grade obstacles, dedicated party rooms, and a layout designed for maximum fun and safety.

👋 Meet the Team Behind Annapolis

DU
Dan Umpa
Owner & Operator

Dan is a military veteran and father who discovered the transformative power of ninja training for his own kids — and decided to bring it to every family in his community. He opened USA Ninja Challenge Stafford in 2025 and grew it from 36 students to over 210 in under a year, making it one of the fastest-growing locations in the entire franchise. Featured on Fox 5's Good Day DC, Dan's vision is simple: build the kind of place where every kid walks in nervous and walks out saying "when can I come back?" Annapolis will be his second owned location, designed from the ground up with everything he's learned about what makes kids (and parents) love coming back week after week.

SS
Stephen Saekoo
Co-Owner & Marketing Partner

Stephen brings a lifetime of working with kids to the ninja world. He took a gap year in college to volunteer at an orphanage in Japan — where he first fell in love with building obstacle courses for children. He later founded an after-school program in Los Angeles to keep kids active and inspired. Now a father of three (including a sensory-seeking child who thrives in ninja training), Stephen acquired USA Ninja Challenge Durham, NC and co-owns the Fairfield, OH location. As co-founder of Second Summit Marketing, he and Dan power the digital strategy behind every USA Ninja location they touch — from lead generation to community building. His mission: create spaces where every child can grow stronger, braver, and more confident.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Do I have to pay anything to pre-register?+

No! Pre-registration is completely free. You're simply reserving your spot and locking in your discount tier. No credit card required, no deposit, no commitment.

What does "20% off for life" actually mean?+

It means exactly that. Founders 50 members pay 20% less than standard tuition every single month, for as long as they remain active members. If our standard monthly rate is $150, you'd pay $120. Forever. The rate is tied to your family account — it never expires and survives any future price increases.

What age groups do you serve?+

We offer classes for kids ages 3 through 17, grouped by age and skill level: Little Ninjas (3-4), Junior Ninjas (5-7), Ninja Training (8-11), and Advanced Ninja (12-17). Every child trains at their appropriate level with age-matched peers.

How does the free birthday party work for the first 5 families?+

The first 5 families to pre-register get one free birthday party (valued at $350+) redeemable within 12 months of our opening. Includes 90 minutes of private obstacle course time for up to 15 kids, a dedicated party host, setup and cleanup, and a party room. You just pick the date!

When exactly do you open?+

We're targeting September 2026. Construction begins in spring. Pre-registered families will get progress updates and first access to class scheduling before we open to the general public.

Where exactly are you located?+

We're at 441 Defense Highway (Route 450) in Annapolis, MD. Easy access from Routes 2, 50, and 97 — convenient for families throughout Anne Arundel County, Crofton, Bowie, Severna Park, and beyond.

Can I register multiple kids?+

Absolutely. Your founding member discount applies to your entire family. Whether you have 1 kid or 4, the discount covers all of them under your family account.
