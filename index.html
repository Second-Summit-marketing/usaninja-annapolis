What Is USA Ninja Challenge?
WHERE KIDS BECOME NINJAS

USA Ninja Challenge is a kids' obstacle course training gym inspired by American Ninja Warrior. Our classes build confidence, strength, coordination, and grit — all while having a blast.

Questions?
FREQUENTLY ASKED
What ages is USA Ninja Challenge for?
Our programs are designed for kids ages 5 to 14. We have different class levels based on age and skill, so every child gets an appropriate challenge whether they're a complete beginner or an experienced athlete.
What does the $49 founding member deposit cover?
The $49 deposit reserves your founding member spot and locks in your lifetime discount. It's applied directly to your first month's tuition — so it's not an extra charge. And it's fully refundable if you change your mind before opening day.
What does "20% off for life" actually mean?
It means exactly that. As a founding member, your monthly tuition rate is locked in at 20% below standard pricing — and it stays that way for as long as you're enrolled. Even if we raise prices in the future, your rate never changes.
When exactly are you opening?
We're targeting September 2026 for our grand opening at 441 Defense Highway in Annapolis. Join the waitlist and we'll keep you updated on the exact date as we get closer.
Do my kids need any prior experience?
Not at all! Our beginner classes start from scratch. Kids learn progressively — grip strength, balance, coordination — and advance at their own pace. Many of our most dedicated ninjas started with zero experience.
Do you offer birthday parties and camps?
Yes! Ninja birthday parties and summer camps are some of our most popular offerings. We'll have more details on party packages and camp schedules as we get closer to opening.
Is joining the waitlist free?
Completely free. No payment info required. You're just reserving your place in line so you get early access to founding member pricing before we open it to the public.
